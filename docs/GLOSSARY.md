# Glossary

A small quant glossary to keep terms consistent across notes and code.

- alpha: excess return over a benchmark.
- beta: exposure to a risk factor (often market).
- IC (information coefficient): correlation between signal and future returns.
- IR (information ratio): mean(active return) / std(active return).
- Sharpe: mean(excess return) / std(excess return).
- max drawdown: max peak-to-trough decline.
- turnover: fraction of portfolio traded per period.
- slippage: execution price impact vs. mid/close/etc.
